The instructor showed us an example of sorting a collection of data using tools such as excel and  google sheets. It was slightly challenging for me as it was my first time using those tools but later I understood the basics of how to use google sheets and excel  by practice. The making of the plots and graphs as a form of representation was interesting for me as I can find information about those things that are useful and important  for me. The instructor made us do everything on our laptops which I found an effective way to teach those things.
