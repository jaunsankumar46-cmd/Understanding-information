My experience on information problems

March 17, 2026

When the topic was introduced by the instructor in the class it was completely new to me but when the instructor started explaining then I realized that it is not new to me. I am already familiar with some of these things. She was teaching us about pixels, the basic unit of images, then how to calculate the storage space required for storing an image ,then how to calculate the storage space required for storing a video . I thought that storing images and videos are simple but then I knew that the storage space of videos depends on the resolution, frames per second, time of the video, and many other things. This is something that I am already a bit familiar with, like I know it would require more space for a 4k 60fps video than a 1080p 30fps video now I know the reason beyond that. The number system conversions are slightly confusing for me in the initial stage but when I solve some problems it becomes familiar for me . The part of fun with images , the konigsberg problems ,knapsack problems was really interesting because it is mostly related to our daily life problems, like we use filters in photos, and the konigsberg problems are like solving a puzzle, but later I understand how to judge whether the konigsberg problem has a solution or not. The number system conversion is something that I am already familiar with in school and also in the course Craft Of Computing in the previous semester but still it is confusing for me ,if I see the problems after a long time. Initially I don’t know why they taught the train graph. Later I think it is related to modeling and abstraction. What I learned in this unit is that we can solve complex problems by representing it in a simple form with the use of modeling, and abstraction and find the best solution using optimization.
