Understanding Information

March 17, 2026

When the topic was first introduced by the instructor in the class I just knew that information is a collection of data. But when the instructor started explaining in detail I realized that it is something deeper than I thought, it is used to gain knowledge by organizing and interpreting data . It may be used to sort and organize a collection of data that can be used to gain knowledge of how to use the information. The instructor showed us an example of sorting a collection of data using tools such as excel and google sheets. It was slightly challenging for me as it was my first time using those tools but later I understood the basics of how to use google sheets and excel by practice. The making of the plots and graphs as a form of representation was interesting for me as I can find information about those things that are useful and important for me. For example, I can plot a graph for my academic performance across the different semesters or academic performance in different courses so that I can keep track of my performance and I can give more time for the course where I found difficulties in learning. The instructor made us do everything on our laptops which I found an effective way to teach those things. I found sometimes it was difficult and sometimes it was easy and I think it is the way of learning . the topic might be useful for me in future for doing some kind of analysis using raw data in google sheets or excel
